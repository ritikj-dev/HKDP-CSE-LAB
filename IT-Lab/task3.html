Hailakandi Polytechnic

About Us

Hailakandi Polytechnic is one of the 5 new polytechnic started their session from October 2020(Academic Year 2020-21). Hailakandi Polytechnic which is under construction in the District of Hailakandi at Katlicherra about 28 km from Hailakandi city, which is southern most city of state Assam.

Hailakandi Polytechnic is an AICTE approved college for Polytechnic and Government institution affiliated to Directorate of Technical Education, Assam situated at Katlicherra, Hailakandi, 788161, Assam.
